Music Projects

I am currently involved in a few music projects:
  • Żibel (Dec 2023-Present) - Thrash metal band based in Sheffield. I play lead guitar for this band and contriubute heavily to our style and direction. Check us out on instagram @zibel.official. The name of the band is a long winded pun. The word Żibel is the Maltese word for garbage, which in american english is reffered to as trash, we play thrash metal which sounds rather similar. We will soon release our first single Scattered Debris.
  • Born Live (Oct 2024 - Present) - This is a Death cover band in which I play bass guitar.
I have also been involved in a number of other music projects in the past:
  • Delirium (Aug 2021 - Sep 2022) - Delirium are a gothic metal band based in Malta. They are still active (as of 2024). I was the only guitarist at the time, and during my time with the band we wrote and recorded a full length album called State of Delirium. Check them out on instagram @delirium_malta. Unfortunately I had to leave this band in September 2022 since I was due to move to Sheffield to start my Ph.D. studies.
  • Mythril (Jan 2017 - Dec 2019) - A thrasm metal band from Malta, who are no longer active. I primarally played rhythm guitar with this band and contributed to writing orignal music. During this period we played a number of shows and recorded one single called Crown of Fire.
  • Stride (2017-2018) - A jazz band that played together and mostly jammed on standards. We once played a wedding, playing a number of jazz standards and pop tunes to get the party going!
